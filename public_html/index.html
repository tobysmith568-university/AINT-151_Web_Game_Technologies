Welcome to: Escape the Hospital!

"You wake up all alone in a silent hospital room...

After 15 minutes there is still no sign of life so you decide to get up and wander around. You don't remember why you're at the hospital and you can't see any sign of injury..."

In this game you must navigate between rooms in order to escape from the hospital. Complete tasks to find items and use those items in specific rooms to gain access to new items and tasks which can help you find your way out.
